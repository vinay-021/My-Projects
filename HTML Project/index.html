Taco Trivia

When did tacos first appear in the United States?

Tacos first appeared in the United States around 1905, when Mexican migrants started arriving to work on railroads and other jobs, bringing their cuisine with them. By the 1920s, these tacos began to be adapted with American ingredients, leading to the "ultimate taco" for many Americans. (Source: twistedtaco )
